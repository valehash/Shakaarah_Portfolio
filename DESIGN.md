# Design System: Shakaaraah Portfolio
**Project ID:** 357423610939880408

## 1. Visual Theme & Atmosphere
The visual theme is **High-Contrast Dark Minimalism** combined with **Flamboyant Editorial Accents** (the "Shakara Effect"). The density is spacious and clean, characterized by structured grid layouts and exposed borders that provide a sense of luxury and structural order, drawing inspiration from high-end agency design portfolios like Outpost.

## 2. Color Palette & Roles
*   **Deep Onyx Base** (`#0A0A0B`): Used for primary page backgrounds to establish depth and high-contrast dark mode.
*   **Charcoal Surface** (`#111113`): Used for card backgrounds, input fields, and form containers to separate content layers.
*   **Vibrant Electric Purple** (`#9B51E0`): Used for primary accent highlights, linear text gradients, focused states, and key call-to-actions.
*   **Neon Amber Gold** (`#FCB900`): Used for secondary accent details, labels, active states, and custom cursor pointer highlights.
*   **Muted Steel Gray** (`#8E919A`): Used for secondary text, descriptions, and structural borders.
*   **Solid White** (`#FFFFFF`): Used for major headlines, headers, and active links.

## 3. Typography Rules
*   **Display/futuristic headers:** **Syne** (bold, geometric letterforms) is used for large hero titles and section headers to create a striking first impression.
*   **Structure/functional headers:** **Outfit** is used for navigation links, buttons, lists, and numbers (`01`, `02`, `03`) to ensure high-end branding clarity.
*   **Body copy:** **Poppins** (regular, 400/500 weight) is used for standard reading paragraphs, FAQ contents, and form inputs to ensure readability.

## 4. Component Stylings
*   **Buttons:**
    *   **Primary Button:** Rounded-pill shape, solid white background with black text. Transitions on hover to Neon Amber Gold (`#FCB900`) with a matching glowing box-shadow and subtle upward transform.
    *   **Secondary Button:** Rounded-pill shape, thin 1px muted white border, transparent background. Transitions on hover to a semi-transparent white background with full border opacity.
*   **Cards/Containers:**
    *   Exposed 1px border cards with transparent backgrounds. Hovering activates a clean, solid Charcoal Surface background (`#111113`) and slides in a purple action arrow.
*   **Inputs/Forms:**
    *   Input boxes feature a dark translucent background with a thin 1px border. Focusing on an input smoothly changes the border color to Vibrant Electric Purple (`#9B51E0`) and adds a subtle glow shadow.

## 5. Layout Principles
*   **Exposed Grid Lines:** Layout modules are divided by a structural 4-column overlay with thin 1px muted borders (`rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.08)`).
*   **Section Headers:** Every major page section begins with an Outpost-inspired, full-width split header: Left side shows a section index (e.g., `01 / SERVICES`) and the title; Right side contains a brief descriptive summary.
*   **Whitespace Strategy:** Large margins and padding (8rem vertical section spacing, 4rem container padding) ensure the layout feels premium, airy, and non-cluttered.
